UofA exists because the credibility frameworks are not the problem. ASME V&V 40, NASA-STD-7009B, and the FDA's 2023 guidance on CM&S credibility all explain *how to assess* simulation credibility. The problem is the last mile: there is no standardized construct for packaging, transmitting, and verifying the *evidence and decisions* those assessments produce. UofA is that construct.
