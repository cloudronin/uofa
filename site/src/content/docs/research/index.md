UofA is the subject of a Doctor of Engineering praxis at George Washington University. The praxis defends three contributions:

- **C1** — Credibility decision as a verifiable artifact (tamper-evident, PROV-DM provenance)
- **C2** — Machine-enforceable evidence completeness via SHACL profiles
- **C3** — Computable quality gates via Jena forward-chaining rules with weakener detection

A coverage methodology, validated against four published defeater taxonomies (Khakzad-Shahandashti, Gohar, Greenwell, Jarzębowicz-Wardziński), establishes that the catalog systematically engages with the prior art rather than assembling patterns ad hoc.
